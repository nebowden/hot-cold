—How to Play "Hot or Cold"—

Can you see the future? Are you able to bend spoons with the power of your very own mind? Of course you can, so this is the game for you. The game selects a random number between 1 and 100—see how quickly you can guess it!

  1. Click on the Start button on the home screen
  2. Enter your guess in the # box and click submit.
  3. You'll get feedback on your guess, from frigid if you are way off, to scorching if you're very close.
  4. Use the feedback to hone in on the number.
  5. All of your prior guesses will be shown so you can see what you've guessed already.
  6. When you guess correctly, you'll see the message correct!
  7. Want to play a new game or start over? Just click "start over" at the bottom of the page.
  8. Now go on and show off your mad guessing skills!
